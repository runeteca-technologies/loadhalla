How It Works

From Missed Call to Captured Opportunity

01

Call Goes Unanswered

A broker calls your business. Your team is unavailable. The call goes to voicemail — or rings out entirely.

02

AI Responds in Seconds

Within seconds, our AI sends a professional, conversational SMS to the caller — not a generic auto-reply.

03

Natural Conversation Begins

The AI asks exactly the right questions to capture every detail — rate, route, time, commodity, and contact info.

04

Your Team Gets Notified

The moment details are captured, your dispatcher gets an instant SMS and dashboard alert with everything needed to act.

05

Zero Opportunities Lost

Every conversation stored, searchable, and actionable. Your team picks up exactly where the AI left off.
